National Historic Registry: Downloaded from NPS on 4/8/2014
National and State Register (History Colorado): Aggregated from History Colorado on 4/8/2014

FEMA Floodplain: Data downloaded from FEMA by request on 10/7/2013. Limited Coverage of Colorado.
CWCB Floodplain: Created by Colorado Water Conservation Board, downloaded by request on 5/5/2014. Represents a 100-Year Flood Plain. Limited Coverage of Colorado.

Wild and Scenic Rivers: digitized by Colorado State Demography Office on 5/2/2014.
Nationwide Rivers Inventory: Data highly modified by the Colorado State Demography Office, based on original data at: NPS.gov. Downloaded on 4/7/2014.
1,000 Ft Buffer for Rivers: created by Colorado State Demography Office on 5/2/2014. Buffers are calculated to be 1,000 feet from a river's centerline. As a result, sites outside of the buffered area could potentially still be within 1,000 feet of the water's edge.

National Wetlands Inventory: Data downloaded from US Fish & Wildlife Service on 4/10/2014. Limited coverage of Colorado.

Endangered Species: Downloaded from US Fish & Wildlife Service Critical Habitat Portal on 4/7/2014.

Airports: digitized by Colorado State Demography Office on 5/15/2014, based on list of airports at the FAA as well as those listed in the National Plan of Integrated Airport Systems (NPIAS).
Airport Buffer Zones: created by Colorado State Demography Office on 5/15/2014. Buffers are 3,000 ft around a civil airport, or 15,000 feet around a military airfield. Contact airport to determine if a site is in a Clear Zone (CZ) or an Accident Potential Zone (APZ).
